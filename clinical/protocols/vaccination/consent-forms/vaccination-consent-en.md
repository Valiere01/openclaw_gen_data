## Part 3: Consent

### I hereby consent to the administration of the vaccine(s) initialed above.

**I acknowledge that:**

1. **I have received** Vaccine Information Statements (VIS) for each vaccine to be administered.

2. **I have had the opportunity** to ask questions and have received satisfactory answers from a healthcare provider.

3. **I understand the benefits** of vaccination, including protection against serious, potentially life-threatening diseases.

4. **I understand the risks** of vaccination, including common side effects (fever, redness, swelling at injection site) and rare but serious adverse reactions.

5. **I understand the risks of NOT vaccinating**, including increased risk of vaccine-preventable diseases for my child and the community.

6. **I authorize** Metropolitan Healthcare Network and its healthcare providers to administer the vaccine(s) to my child.

7. **I consent** to the reporting of vaccine administration to the state/local immunization registry as required by law.

8. **I understand** that I may withdraw consent at any time before vaccine administration.
